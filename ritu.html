How to make pakodas?

  1. Begin with gram flour.
  2. Add some Indian spices to it like Chillies ,Garlic ,Cumin powder,Corriender powder etc.
  3. Mix with some water to get a thick slurry.
  4. Toss sliced potatoes in it and take out in boiling oil.
  5. Fry till golden color and then take them out.
  6. Pakodas are ready to serve with some green chutney or ketchup.

Scrambled eggs

No need to sat how much I love eggs. They have always been protein power house for me.

Ingredients

  • Eggs ,obviously.
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Cream
Recipe
  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl and mix it with cream to get a thick batter.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan .
    1. While doing so keep in mind that butter may get burned if exposed to high temperature.
    2. Rather prefer to do it with some oil in it.
    3. Also special care of the temperature of room should be taken. Must not be too cold or too warm.
  3. Add batter of eggs and cream in the pan and keep mixing it using cut and fold technique.
  4. For each 20 seconds ,give it a stir and take it off the flame even if it's still moist.
    • It will keep on cooking until you serve it. The heat trapped inside is your Friend or foe .
  5. Serve it with some chopped celery and green onions.
